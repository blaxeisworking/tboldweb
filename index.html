How it works

Trash collection within 3 easy steps!

01

Dustbin gets filled

Trashcan gets filled and locks itself which can't be unlocked without authentication. It sends notification to the application.

02

Notifications

The app will show notification to user and the admin. The admin will recieve notifications for trashcan under their juridiction and the user will recive it for their own particular area.

03

Trash collected

Assigned user goes and unlock the bins with help of a particular authentication key, and the trash gets collected.
